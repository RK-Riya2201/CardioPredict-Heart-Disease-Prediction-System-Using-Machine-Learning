About CardioPredict

CardioPredict is an machine learning-based web application to predict heart disease risk. It leverages clinical data and machine learning to provide early detection and preventive insights.

Input Features

  • Age, Sex: Basic demographic information.
  • Chest Pain Type (cp): Typical/atypical/non-anginal/asymptomatic.
  • Resting Blood Pressure (trestbps): Measured in mm Hg.
  • Serum Cholesterol (chol): Measured in mg/dl.
  • Fasting Blood Sugar (fbs): >120 mg/dl (1) or ≤120 mg/dl (0).
  • Resting ECG Results (restecg): Normal, ST-T abnormality, or LV hypertrophy.
  • Maximum Heart Rate (thalach): Highest heart rate during exercise.
  • Exercise Induced Angina (exang): Yes (1) or No (0).
  • ST Depression (oldpeak): Exercise-induced vs rest.
  • ST Segment Slope (slope): Upsloping, flat, or downsloping.
  • Major Vessels (ca): 0–3 colored by fluoroscopy.
  • Thalassemia (thal): Normal, fixed, or reversible defect.

How It Works

CardioPredict uses a trained machine learning model. Clinical parameters are analyzed to provide risk assessment and guidance. This tool supports, not replaces, professional medical advice.

Benefits

  • Early identification of heart risks.
  • Informed health and lifestyle decisions.
  • Support for healthcare professionals.
  • Promotes preventive care and awareness.
